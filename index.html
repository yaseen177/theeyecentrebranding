Our Visual Identity

Welcome to the official brand guidelines for the eye centre. This manual outlines the core elements of our visual identity, ensuring consistency, clarity, and recognition across all our communications. By adhering to these rules, we maintain a cohesive and professional brand presence.

3. Typography

Primary Brand Typeface

Quicksand

Quicksand is a geometric sans-serif with rounded terminals. It mirrors the circular and friendly nature of our logomark. We use it for our logotype, all primary headings, and promotional call-outs.

4. Usage & Clear Space

Clear Space Minimum

To ensure the logo remains clear and legible, always maintain a minimum clear space around it. This space is defined by "x", which is equal to half the height of the circular logomark.

x
x

Minimum Size

To maintain legibility, never reproduce the logo smaller than the dimensions specified below across print and digital mediums.

Digital: 120px wide
Print: 30mm wide
Digital: 24px wide
Print: 8mm wide
