Logo Usage Rules

Follow these rules to keep the brand consistent and professional across every touchpoint.

Do

Use the teal circle with white spectacles as the primary brand mark on all touchpoints

Don't

Place the logo on busy photographic backgrounds without a clear exclusion zone

Do

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the circle on all four sides at minimum

Don't

Stretch, skew, rotate, recolour, or apply effects to any part of the logo

Do

Use the fully white reversed version when placing on the teal primary background

Don't

Use the icon mark below 24px rendered size — it loses legibility at very small sizes

Do

Use DM Serif Display for the wordmark only — never substitute with other typefaces

Don't

Place the wordmark without the icon mark except where space constraints require it
